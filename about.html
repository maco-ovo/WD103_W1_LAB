Our Story

Tractor was founded by Meghan and Steve Clarke with a simple goal: to create a place where people could eat healthy, delicious food every day. We wanted to offer nutritious meals that don't compromise on taste, replacing greasy, heavy fast food with fresh, high-quality ingredients.

Our Food Philosophy

  • Clean Eating: No hidden ingredients, just fresh, whole foods.
  • Everyday Accessibility: Healthy meals that are quick and easy to grab on the go.
  • Better Sourcing: Working with farmers and suppliers who care about quality as much as we do.
